Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about MOQ, packaging, lead time, export documentation, and quality standards for dehydrated powders and custom blends.

What is your MOQ for bulk orders?

MOQ depends on product and packing format. Share product, grade/mesh, packaging, monthly volume and destination — we’ll confirm MOQ & availability.

Do you support different mesh sizes and granulation?

Yes. We can supply powders, flakes and granules. We confirm specs (mesh/grade) before bulk production.

Do you offer packaging options and private label?

We support bulk/export packaging formats. Private label can be discussed based on quantity, destination and compliance needs.

Are your products suitable for export?

Yes. We support export-friendly labeling & packing guidance and documentation on request.

Can you share specification sheets (moisture, mesh, etc.)?

Yes. Share the product and intended application and we’ll share typical specs and confirm against your requirement.

How do you ensure consistent batch quality?

We confirm sample + specs before bulk supply and follow consistency checks per production lot.

Still have a question?

Share your product, grade/mesh, MOQ, packaging preference, and destination. We’ll respond with a bulk quote and lead time.
