FAQs

Quick answers to common bulk buying questions for dehydrated products, spray dried ingredients and spices.

Bulk buying & ordering

What is your MOQ for bulk orders?
MOQ depends on product, grade (powder/flakes/granules) and packaging. Share your requirement on WhatsApp and we’ll confirm MOQ and pricing.
Do you support different mesh sizes and granulation?
Yes. We support multiple mesh sizes/grades based on application. Tell us your use-case (soups, seasoning, HORECA, processing) and we’ll suggest the best option.
Do you offer packaging options and private label?
We support bulk/export packs and can support OEM / private label based on product and volume.
Are your products suitable for export?
Yes. We support export-ready documentation and quality consistency based on requirement. Share destination & compliance needs to confirm.

Quality & compliance

Can you share specification sheets (moisture, mesh, etc.)?
Yes. Tell us the product and grade required and we’ll share available specifications.
How do you ensure consistent batch quality?
We follow standard process controls and batch checks. For sensitive applications, we can align to your specs and sampling requirements.
